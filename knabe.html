Biography

Míle focuses his practice on complex business litigation, including contract and real estate issues, employment disputes, and business torts. Míle also represents start-ups and established businesses in corporate transactions, entity choice, formation, and compliance.

Having previously worked with one of Chicago’s premier family law firms, Míle has extensive experience in the litigation and settlement of sensitive issues. In his prior practice, Míle worked closely with high net-worth individuals and their spouses to put them and their families in the best position to achieve their personal and professional goals post-separation.

Míle began working with Marcus & Boxerman in 2014 as a law clerk and re-joined the firm in 2018 after several years in private family law practice.
