CORPORATE & BUSINESS ATTORNEYS

From your company's formation forward, we provide the legal expertise your business needs to thrive. We pride ourselves on understanding every facet of your business, planning for the unexpected, and protecting what you’ve built. Whether you have one employee or one thousand, you have a legal team with the right skills.

We excel in negotiating and drafting commercial contracts, advising small business owners on business type and structure, closing transactions, and resolving disputes both within the company and with third parties.

Our corporate and business work includes

  • Serving as general counsel to small and medium-sized businesses
  • Advising on business entity choice and structure
  • Forming and organizing corporations, limited liability companies and other business entities
  • Drafting by-laws, operating agreements and shareholders’ agreements
  • Representing buyers and sellers of businesses in stock and asset sale transactions
  • Reviewing and advising on loan and other bank documents
  • Purchasing, selling and leasing commercial and industrial real estate
  • Resolving partnership and shareholder disputes, director and shareholder deadlock and shareholder “freeze-outs”
  • Drafting non-compete, confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements
  • Maintaining business records for regulatory compliance
